What was DIVX

  • Rental Format Variation of DVD's
  • Approx. $4.50/48hr Rental
  • Tiered Membership System

Shortcomings

-Pan and Scan

-Limited special features

-Negative labeling of Standard DVD's as "Basic DVD"

Why did DIVX Fail?

-Costs of format introduction

-General Public did not embrace it over DVD

-BlockBuster did not want to carry DIVX DVD's

-Over 330 Million dollars lost
